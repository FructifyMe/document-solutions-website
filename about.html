Document Solutions is an independent Canon authorized dealer based in Beverly, Massachusetts. The company sells, installs, leases, and services the Canon imageRUNNER and imageCLASS product lines for businesses across the North Shore.

Owner Jim Cross brings more than 30 years of office equipment experience to a small, factory-trained team. Document Solutions holds an A+ rating with the Better Business Bureau and counts long-tenured local customers — including Century 21 North East, Manchester Athletic Club, and Excel Paving — among the businesses it serves.

Document Solutions also operates as NJM Consulting — Jim's workflow consulting practice for businesses that want to go beyond hardware into document management. NJM engagements deploy Canon uniFLOW print management software for secure print release, scan-to-cloud workflows, and per-user accounting.

You can reach Jim and the team at 978-750-0444, by email at jim@documentsolution.org, or in person at 8 Garfield Avenue, Beverly, MA 01915.

What we believe

Three things we don't compromise on.

Show up.

When your machine is down you're losing money. Our Canon factory-trained technicians are dispatched out of Beverly so they can be on the road quickly with the right parts in the truck.

Never sell what you don't need.

We routinely talk customers down a tier when their volume doesn't justify a bigger machine. The long relationship matters more than this quarter.

Own the whole problem.

Hardware, supplies, software, lease structure, end-of-term — one accountable team. You don't get bounced between your dealer, your service vendor, and your IT consultant.
