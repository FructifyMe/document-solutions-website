Document Solutions was founded in Beverly, Massachusetts in 2005 by Jim Cross — but the story really starts in 1996, when Jim sold his first copier. Same customer base, same handshake, just better equipment now.

JC
Jim Cross

30+ years in industry Canon factory-trained

Most of our customers found us by word of mouth — and most of them have been with us for more than a decade. There's a reason for that.

Jim started in office equipment in the mid-1990s, working for a regional dealer covering the same territory we serve today. By the time he'd been at it ten years, he'd watched the industry consolidate around two patterns he didn't like: salespeople who disappeared after the install, and service organizations that punted easy problems back to the customer.

In 2005 he founded Document Solutions on the simple bet that a small, factory-trained, owner-operated dealership could outperform the big regionals on the things that actually matter to a 25-person law firm or a 50-bed medical practice — response time, accountability, and knowing the customer's machine without having to look up the serial number.

Twenty years later, the same playbook still wins. We're a four-person operation. Our techs are Canon factory-trained. Our customer list runs from Manchester-by-the-Sea to Newburyport. We hold a BBB A+ rating, and our oldest active customer relationships are now in their second decade.

"We win on speed, accuracy, and the fact that when you call, you're not call number 47 in a queue. That's not a marketing line — it's a structural advantage of being small and local."

Document Solutions also operates as NJM Consulting — Jim's workflow consulting practice, where we help businesses redesign how their documents move through their organization using Canon uniFLOW print management software. That's where the deepest cost savings usually live, and it's a side of the business that grows every year.

What we believe
