Common questions

Managed print, demystified.

What's included in a managed print agreement?
Canon hardware (lease or owned), all toner and consumables (drums, fusers, waste containers), automatic supply shipments, remote device monitoring, all on-site service labor and parts, and consolidated per-page billing across every device on the agreement.
How much does it cost on the North Shore?
Per-page pricing typically runs $0.008–$0.015 for black-and-white and $0.045–$0.085 for color, depending on monthly volume, device tier, and contract length. Most customers cut their total print spend by 20–35% in year one. Our TCO calculator will give you a real number against your actual setup.
Do I have to lease the equipment through Document Solutions?
No. We support managed print on equipment you already own, equipment leased through us, or a mix. Most customers lease through us because the per-month financing rolls into the same invoice as supplies and service — but it's not required.
What happens when toner runs low?
Each device reports supply levels back to our monitoring system. When toner crosses about 12–15% remaining, a replacement cartridge ships automatically to your office, usually arriving within two business days. You stop ordering, you stop running out.
How fast is service when something breaks?
Most North Shore service tickets get on-site response within 4 business hours. Roughly 92% of issues are resolved on the first visit — because our techs are Canon factory-trained and stock common parts in the service truck.
Can you take over a competitor's contract mid-term?
Yes. We routinely transition customers from competitors. We can help model an early-buyout when the math works, or structure the new agreement to start cleanly when the existing one ends — typically 90–120 days out. No service gap.
