How the math works.

No black boxes. Here's exactly what this calculator does — and what it can't do without a real assessment.

Inputs we use

Device count, monthly mono and color volumes, current lease cost, supply spend, service-call frequency, and equipment age. Those are the variables that drive 90% of total print cost.

Hidden costs we add back

Toner stockouts, emergency service surcharges, downtime hours, and the staff time spent ordering supplies. These are real costs that almost never make it into a copier RFP.
